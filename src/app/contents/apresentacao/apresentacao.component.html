Novidades!!!

Spotify Search and Favorite


This WebSite was created with the purpose of optimizing the search of artists, albums and songs, facilitating and entertaining the search with style and good intuition.


Features!!!


On the music page you can see the enlarged album image.

On the artist page you can see the artist's picture enlarged.

Still on the artists page you can click on the artist name and check the last five albums produced .

On the album page you can see the enlarged album image .

Favor your favorite albums.

Still on the album page you can click on the album name and have access to the preview of the songs!!!


Have fun!!!
